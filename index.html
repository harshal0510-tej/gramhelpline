About GramConnect

Bridging the Gap Between Villagers and Panchayat

GramConnect is a government-backed digital initiative that brings transparency and accountability to Gram Panchayat functioning. Villagers no longer need to travel long distances or wait for weekly meetings to report issues.

Every registered village gets its own dashboard, making complaints visible to the right authorities who are responsible for resolution.

  • ✅ Paperless complaint management
  • ✅ Dedicated village spaces
  • ✅ Transparent resolution tracking
  • ✅ Works on low-end smartphones
  • ✅ Available in regional languages (coming soon)
